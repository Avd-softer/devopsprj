History of AI

AI research began in the 1950s. Alan Turing proposed that machines could think. John McCarthy coined the term "Artificial Intelligence" in 1956 at the Dartmouth Conference.

The Future of AI

AI will power advanced robotics, real-time translation, and decision-making systems in industries like healthcare, agriculture, and space exploration.

Benefits of AI

  • Automation of repetitive tasks
  • Increased efficiency and productivity
  • Enhanced data analysis and predictions
  • Improved user experiences
