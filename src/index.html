What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to mimic human intelligence. It enables computers and robots to solve problems, learn, and make decisions without human input.

Popular Applications of AI

  • Chatbots and Virtual Assistants
  • Autonomous Vehicles
  • Medical Diagnostics
  • Facial Recognition Systems
  • Recommendation Engines (e.g., Netflix, Amazon)
